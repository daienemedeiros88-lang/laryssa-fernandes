Common Questions

FAQ

Where do the sessions take place?
Sessions take place in Faro, Portugal. The exact location will be confirmed upon booking. Online coaching is available for remote clients.
Do I need previous gym experience?
Not at all. The Foundations programme is specifically designed for beginners or those returning to training. Laryssa will meet you exactly where you are.
How do I get started?
Simply reach out via the Contact page. Laryssa will get back to you to discuss your goals and recommend the best programme for you.
Can I switch programmes later?
Absolutely. As your fitness evolves, your programme can evolve with you. Laryssa will guide you through any transitions.
