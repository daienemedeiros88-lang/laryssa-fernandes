Her Story

Passion turned profession

Laryssa Fernandes has been passionate about sport from a young age, beginning her journey as part of an athletics team and developing a lifelong commitment to movement and training. What started as a personal passion naturally evolved into a professional path.

She completed a two-year Personal Training course with a clear intention: to help others feel stronger, more confident, and more connected to their bodies. At just 27, Laryssa brings both energy and dedication to her work, continually striving to grow her knowledge and refine her approach — with plans to pursue further training and development in the near future.

Her coaching style is built on three core principles: structure, support, and sustainability. Every programme is designed around the individual — because no two people have the same goals, lifestyle, or starting point.
